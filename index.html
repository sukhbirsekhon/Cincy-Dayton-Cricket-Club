About

Cincinnati Dayton Cricket Club (CDCC) is a team of cricketers who play together a game of Cricket. Cricket is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams of eleven players each on a field at the center of which is a 22-yard (20-meter) pitch with a wicket at each end, each comprising two bails balanced on three stumps. We play throughout the year in different formats such as T20 and T35. We have a set of dedicated players who have been playing for the team for more than 10 years, but we encourage new players to join who showcase excellent game performance along with team-player skills. We have great mentors in our team who likes to share their experience to help the team achieve their maximum potential on the field. We play at least twice a week including practice to keep us in the best shape throughout the year.

Our team focuses on utilizing each player's unique strengths on the field and working on improving weaknesses off the field. We build relationships with our players through social gatherings to maintain a positive and light environment in the team. Everyone is encouraged to provide their insights in team meetings to ensure everyone has their say in team decisions. We are a well-established cricket team in the midwest region and we have played with a lot of teams and players. Feel free to contact us and join our practices.
